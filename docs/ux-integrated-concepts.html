‚úì Progressive Disclosure Pattern
Three levels of information:
1. Default: One-line summary (fastest scan)
2. Hover: Preview snippet + matched keywords highlighted + query that matched
3. Click: Full GitLab context

Keyboard equivalent: Focus (Tab) triggers same tooltip as hover. Press 'e' to expand inline, 'o' to open in GitLab.
How Hover Works

Mouse: Hover for 300ms ‚Üí Tooltip appears

Keyboard: Tab to item ‚Üí Tooltip appears

Why it works: Dense scanning uninterrupted, context available when you need it

Matched keywords: Highlighted in preview to show why this item matched your query
